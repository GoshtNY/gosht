Who we are

About

Gosht is a fast-casual gastrobistro specializing in meat dishes. The assortment of steaks is represented by premium cuts, and also includes alternative types of cuts. This choice allows you to cover a wider market segment. Founded in 2016 in Tashkent, Gosht has become a real discovery for all connoisseurs of meat thanks to the high quality of the ingredients used, an experienced team of chefs, as well as the unique technology of cooking meat on charcoal on the Spanish Josper grill. Hence the slogan of the restaurant "Main for meat". The "craft" menu, a wide range of dishes and an aﬀordable price have becomethe hallmark of the brand, thanks to which the Gosht project consistently takes prizes at local HORECA festivals.

Josper oven

Our outlets are equipped with a closed grill oven from the Spanish manufacturer Zozreg. The uniqueness of this oven lies in the fact that it retains the exceptional texture and juiciness of dishes, giving them the special aroma inherent in dishes cookedon charcoal.
